About Me

My name is Jason Sangiamo and I am a junior student at Washington University in Saint Louis studying computer science with a secondary major in philosophy-neuroscience-psychology. My expected graduation date is in May 2020


I aspire to be a software engineer after I graduate, ideally in some capacity that synthesizes my two majors. I view my studies in philosophy-neuroscience-psychology as an education in a holistic understanding of people and computer science as a tool with which I can leverage that understanding to create a better product.
